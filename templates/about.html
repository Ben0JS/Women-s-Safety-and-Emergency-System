About Women Safety Web App

This platform is developed to help women stay safe by enabling smart emergency features such as real-time location sharing, alert systems, and secure login. It empowers users to act quickly during unsafe situations.

Key Features

Real-time location tracking
Emergency alert button
Fake call & SOS simulation
Video recording
History of previous alerts

Mission Statement

Our mission is to use technology to create safer environments for women everywhere.
